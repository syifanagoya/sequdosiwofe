Biography

Isaac Herzog was born in Israel on September 22, 1960. Israeli politician who became a member of the Knesset in 2003, serving as chairman of the Labor Party. In 2013, he became Leader of the Opposition in the Knesset. He began working alongside Benjamin Netanyahu, who became Israel’s Prime Minister for the second time in 2009. He received his education from Tel Aviv University, then went to work for his father’s law firm, Herzog, Fox & Ne’eman.
