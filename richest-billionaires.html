What is Peter Woo's Net Worth?

Peter Woo is a Hong Kong-based businessman who has a net worth of $13 billion. Peter Woo has accumulated most of his wealth as the Chairman of Wheelock & Company Limited and The Wharf Holdings Limited, which contains his main real estate, investment, logistics and media businesses. As for Wheelock & Co, he manages the company through his 59.8% stake. Among notable prizes in Wharf's property portfolio are New York City's Times Square (the top venue for the New Year's Eve) and Kowloon's Harbour City. Furthermore, he owns a minority stake in Salvatore Ferragamo and 73% of fashion retailer Joyce Boutique. Thanks to his business commitment, he was named "Leader of the Year" by the Hong Kong Standard in 1995. Apart from being one of the notable Hong Kong's businessmen, he is currently a married man with three children.

Net Worth details: Peter Woo owns 61 percent of property developer Wheelock & Co. He also owns the largest malls in Hong Kong, Times Square and the Harbour City.
