Jessica Ciola Dies in a Fatal Car Crash

The community of Bethany, Connecticut is mourning the loss of beloved resident Jessica Ciola, who tragically lost her life in a car accident in Woodbridge.

The 46-year-old’s unexpected demise has left her family, friends, and community in shock and deep sorrow.

The fatal incident occurred on North Pease Road, between Fairgrounds Road and Amity Road. Law enforcement authorities are investigating the circumstances surrounding the accident. Preliminary reports indicate that Ciola’s vehicle veered off the roadway into a wooded area, resulting in a collision with a tree.

Jessica Ciola was a cherished member of the Bethany community, known for her kindness and positivity. Her untimely passing leaves a void in the hearts of those who knew her. As we grapple with this tragic news, our deepest condolences go out to her family and friends.

This tragedy serves as a stark reminder of the importance of road safety. We urge everyone to exercise caution and stay safe on the roads, especially during these winter months when driving conditions can be unpredictable.
