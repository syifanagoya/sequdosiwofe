Alexa Carole Havins (born November 16, 1980) is an American actress who has been in a number of films. She initially gained widespread attention in 2003, when she was cast in the character of Babe Carey Chandler on the television soap opera All My Children, which she originated. Her portrayal of the imperfect but good Babe Carey garnered her a nomination for a Daytime Emmy Award in 2005, as well as distinction as one half of one of the show’s most popular soap opera partnerships at the time of nomination. Havins has been in a number of television episodes as well as a number of indie films, all of which have received positive reviews. The following year, she joined the cast of Torchwood’s fourth season, Torchwood: Miracle Day, which aired on BBC Worldwide and the premium television network Starz in the United States.
