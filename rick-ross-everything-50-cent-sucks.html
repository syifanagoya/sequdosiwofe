Rick Ross Thinks Everything 50 Cent Does Sucks

Rick Ross‘ disliking for 50 Cent is showing no signs of stopping anytime soon, revealing that he’s not messing with anything the G-Unit mogul is behind.

During a sprawling new interview with Rolling Stone, Rozay admitted he thinks pretty much everything 50 does these days “sucks.”

AD LOADING...

“Everything 50 [Cent] does sucks,” Ross said when the interviewer threw dirt on 50 Cent’s Get Rich or Die Tryin’ biopic.

The journalist explained that the director Jim Sheridan’s career was allegedly finished after directing the 2005 film.

“R.I.P. Look what 50 Cent did to you,” he responded. “But at least he got a movie done about him. He took advantage of the system. But oh, my God, please! Who wants to see a movie about 50?!”

AD LOADING...

Despite Rick Ross’ comments, there’s one 50 Cent-featured track that the Biggest Bawse was complimentary about during a new interview with Complex, The Game’s “Hate It Or Love It.”

“I mentioned his mom,” Ross said. “She was one of those. So I said it in front of the song but the song was produced by Cool & Dre, my homies. So the song was a dope song. But I’ma shit on homie. He ain’t getting no money. If he ever come here ask him, ‘How it feel Rozay getting all that money?'”

AD LOADING...

Rozay added that he doesn’t see his feud with Fif coming to an end anytime soon. “I doubt it,” he said of squashing their beef. “We ain’t got no jobs for him or nothing. I’m sure he do [need a job].”

50 and Rick Ross have been embroiled in a feud dating back to the late 2000s. The fallout reportedly began in 2008 when 50 allegedly gave Rozay the ice grill at an event. 50 later denied that this encounter ever took place.

In 2009, Ross released “Mafia Music,” where he dissed 50, mentioning Shaniqua Tompkins, the mother of 50’s child, who had to evacuate a house due to a fire that the G-Unit rapper owned.  In response, 50 released “Officer Ricky (Go Head, Try Me).” The beef got extremely personal with 50 releasing a sex tape featuring Ross’ baby mother.
