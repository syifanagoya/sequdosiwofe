Kanye West reveals he is a Justin Bieber fan and proves it by singing his song

    Kanye West is a belieber! The rapper has revealed that Justin Bieber's single was his favourite song of 2015, and proved it by singing along in a video shared on his wife Kim Kardashian's Snapchat account.

    The father-of-two made the surprising revelation on Twitter on Monday, telling fans: "What do you mean by @JustinBieber was my favourite song of 2015."

    View post on Instagram 

    Kanye West sang along to Justin Bieber on Kim Kardashian's Snapchat account

    Just hours later Kanye proved his point by singing along to the track while driving with Kim, who posted the video on her new Snapchat account. The star has been entertaining fans with a number of clips starring Kanye, herself and their daughter North since she signed up to the app last week, although typically they have only been listening to Kanye's new album .

    Justin appeared to be flattered by Kanye's comments and posted a screenshot of his tweet on Instagram on Tuesday morning. The 22-year-old is mutually appreciative of Kanye's work and admitted to being left speechless while working with the rapper in the past.

    Kanye West has revealed he is a fan of Justin Bieber

    "Kanye's a talker," Justin told French website about their collaboration in 2015. "You get him in a room and he starts talking and ranting, I just sit and I listen. He's been through a lot of stuff and he's not just talking to talk, he has something to say from his heart. I just sit and listen because he's really smart."

    Justin is not the only person to observe Kanye's outspoken nature. The 38-year-old has been voicing his opinions and feelings in a number of now infamous Twitter outbursts in recent weeks, prompting his mother-in-law Kris Jenner to joke about grounding him.

    Speaking during an appearance on in February, Kris said: "There have been days where there should be a no tweeting law. I feel like I will ground him."
