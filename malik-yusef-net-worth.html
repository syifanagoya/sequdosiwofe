What is Malik Yusef's Net Worth?

Malik Yusef is an American spoken word artist, poet, film producer, actor, and musician who has a net worth of $4 million. Malik Yusef was born in Chicago, Illinois in April 1971. He became friends with rapper Common as a child and became a street poet. Malik Yusef has released the albums The Great Chicago Fire; A Cold Day in Hell in 2003 and G.O.O.D.

Morning, G.O.O.D. Night in 2009. He has released the singles "Wouldn't You Like to Ride" featuring Kanye West and Common and "Magic Men" featuring Kanye West, Common, and John Legend. Yusef has also collaborated on the songs "My City" by Common, "Crack Music" featuring Common & Kanye West, "Stay Up" by Kanye West featuring 88-Keys, "Promised Land" by Kanye West, Adam Levine, and John Legend, "Ayyy Girl" by JYJ featuring Kanye West, and "Nobody's Smiling" by Common.
