Ashley Scott’s Career Overview

Ashley Scott made her film debut as Gigolo Jane in A.I. Artificial Intelligence in 2001. From there, she took on notable roles in Walking Tall, Into the Blue, The Kingdom, and 12 Rounds.

Her television career includes significant roles in Dark Angel as Asha Barlow, Birds of Prey as Helena Kyle / Huntress, and Jericho as Emily Sullivan.

Scott is also known for her voice work, particularly as Maria Miller in the video games The Last of Us and The Last of Us Part II.
